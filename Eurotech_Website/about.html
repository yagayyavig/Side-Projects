About Eurotech Canada

Welcome to Eurotech Canada, a trusted name in ISO certifications, compliance, and auditing services. We are proud to be a sister concern of Eurocert S.A., a globally recognized and accredited certification body headquartered in Athens, Greece. Since its inception in 1998, Eurocert S.A. has been at the forefront of third-party inspections and certifications, building a legacy of trust and excellence across industries.

At Eurotech Canada, established in 2018, we bring this international expertise to businesses in Canada, the USA, and select global markets. Our focus is on providing internationally recognized ISO Certifications, CE Certifications, and Social & Labour Assessment Auditing to help organizations achieve operational excellence and global recognition.

What We Do

We specialize in helping businesses unlock their potential by attaining globally respected certifications. Our services are designed to:

  • Improve workplace quality and efficiency.
  • Build trust and credibility with customers and partners.
  • Strengthen supply chain metrics and meet tendering contract requirements.

By offering clear and unbiased certification processes, we enable small and medium-sized businesses to stand out in their industries and inspire confidence through demonstrated commitment to quality and compliance.

Our Mission
