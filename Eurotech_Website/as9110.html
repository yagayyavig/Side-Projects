AS 9110 C Certification

Aerospace Maintenance Organization

About AS 9110 C

AS 9110 C is a specialized aerospace quality management standard developed for Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) organizations. It expands on ISO 9001 and includes additional requirements focused on airworthiness, safety, and maintenance documentation integrity.

Tailored for civil and military aviation MRO providers, AS 9110 C ensures that aircraft maintenance services are performed in compliance with aviation regulations and industry best practices, including those from the FAA, EASA, and other national authorities.

Key Components of AS 9110 C

  • Safety Management System (SMS): Promotes proactive identification and control of risks affecting aviation safety and airworthiness.
  • Maintenance Documentation Control: Requirements for ensuring that technical records, logs, and work orders are accurate, up to date, and traceable.
  • Personnel Qualifications: Verifies licensing, certification, and training of technicians, inspectors, and quality personnel.
  • Work Planning & Job Cards: Ensures structured maintenance workflows including detailed inspection instructions, parts usage, and repair procedures.
  • Regulatory Conformance: Addresses adherence to local and international aviation authorities such as FAA, EASA, and Transport Canada.
  • Human Factors: Includes guidance for reducing human error, fatigue, and miscommunication in maintenance tasks.
  • Continual Airworthiness Monitoring: Includes mechanisms to maintain airworthiness of aircraft and components after service.

Why AS 9110 C Matters

  • Reduces operational and safety risks in aircraft maintenance services.
  • Helps meet regulatory and contractual MRO compliance requirements.
  • Improves customer trust and credibility in aviation services.
  • Enables qualification for defense and civil aviation contracts globally.
  • Standardizes inspection, documentation, and airworthiness procedures.
  • Supports inclusion in the IAQG OASIS database for global recognition.
