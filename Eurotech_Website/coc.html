Code of Conduct Audit

Assesses supplier compliance with corporate codes of conduct

About Code of Conduct Audits

A Code of Conduct audit assesses a company’s compliance with ethical, legal, and social expectations as outlined by their clients, industry standards, or internal policies. These audits are often requested by international buyers to ensure responsible sourcing practices and social accountability.

Eurotech Canada conducts Code of Conduct audits based on globally recognized frameworks such as ETI Base Code, ILO conventions, and client-specific standards. These audits verify that suppliers uphold fair labor practices, health and safety requirements, and ethical operations throughout the supply chain.

Key Components of Code of Conduct Audits

  • Working Conditions: Evaluation of working hours, rest periods, wages, and overtime practices.
  • Child & Forced Labor: Verifies compliance with laws prohibiting child labor and forced labor of any kind.
  • Health & Safety: Assessment of the workplace for hazards, emergency protocols, and sanitation.
  • Freedom of Association: Ensures workers’ rights to form unions and engage in collective bargaining.
  • Discrimination & Harassment: Reviews anti-discrimination policies and enforcement of equal opportunity standards.
  • Disciplinary Practices: Audits how misconduct is addressed and whether disciplinary actions are fair and documented.
  • Management Systems: Evaluates documentation, grievance mechanisms, and training related to Code of Conduct enforcement.

Why Code of Conduct Audits Matter

  • Demonstrates social responsibility and alignment with global ethical standards.
  • Fulfills client requirements for ethical sourcing and supply chain transparency.
  • Reduces risk of supply chain disruption due to labor violations or reputational damage.
  • Enhances brand trust and public perception in international markets.
  • Supports worker welfare and promotes fair employment practices.
  • Builds eligibility for SEDEX, SA8000, and other social audit frameworks.

Eurotech Canada helps organizations implement, document, and validate their Code of Conduct practices through thorough audits and compliance support.
