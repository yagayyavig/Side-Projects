Our Certification Process

From your initial inquiry to ongoing surveillance, Eurotech ensures a transparent, compliant, and streamlined certification journey.

1. Quotation and Charges

EUROTECH provides detailed quotations based on IAF guidelines and published fee schedules. All charges are transparent and reviewed with the client.

2. Application

Clients submit an application form covering all site locations and standards. EUROTECH reviews and assigns a lead auditor.

3. Introductory Visit

An optional visit where a lead auditor explains the registration process and performs a facility walkthrough.

4. Practice Assessment

An unofficial, optional pre-assessment to help identify readiness without affecting certification decisions.

5. Documented Information Review

Review of quality/environmental manuals to assess compliance with applicable standards.

6. Readiness Review

A detailed pre-audit check confirming internal audits and management reviews are complete and conforming.

7. Registration Assessment

On-site audit assessing conformity to ISO or other applicable standards. Nonconformities are classified and reported.

8. Corrective Action / Follow-up Visit

Client addresses nonconformities. Follow-up visits may be required to verify resolution.

9. Registration Grant Decision

Certification decision is made based on audit results and successful implementation of corrective actions.

10. On-going Surveillance

Annual audits verify continued conformity. Complaints and improvement records are reviewed.

11. Re-Certification Audit

Every 3 years, a full re-assessment is conducted to maintain certification validity.
