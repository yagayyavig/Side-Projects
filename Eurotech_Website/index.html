About Us

Eurotech Assessment Services Inc. is a Canadian certification body empowering businesses through globally recognized ISO, CE, and Social Audit certifications. Backed by our partnership with Eurocert S.A., we combine international standards with Canadian values to help organizations achieve compliance, credibility, and growth.

With decades of experience and a global presence, we simplify complex certification journeys for businesses across sectors like aerospace, construction, and manufacturing.

Why Choose Us

  • Decades of industry expertise
  • Globally recognized certifications
  • Dedicated support through the entire audit process
  • Fast and reliable assessment services

Our Global Presence

Eurotech Canada operates on a global scale, delivering internationally recognized certification and audit services to clients across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. With a strong presence in over 25 countries, we support a wide range of industries in meeting rigorous international compliance standards.

Our global network allows us to understand regional regulatory environments while maintaining consistency with ISO, CE, and Social Audit frameworks. From large-scale manufacturers in Germany and India to construction leaders in Canada and the UAE, our clients benefit from localized expertise and a trusted international reputation.

Wherever your business operates, Eurotech Canada brings the tools, expertise, and certification pathways to help you compete globally, meet market demands, and grow with confidence.
