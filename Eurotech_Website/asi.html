About ASI Certification

The Aluminium Stewardship Initiative (ASI) is a global certification standard promoting responsible production, sourcing, and stewardship of aluminium throughout the value chain. ASI aims to enhance sustainability and human rights practices in mining, refining, smelting, and manufacturing processes.

ASI Certification helps businesses demonstrate their commitment to ethical and sustainable aluminium sourcing. Eurotech Canada assists organizations in aligning with ASI standards and preparing for independent audits.

Key Components of ASI Certification

  • Environmental Responsibility: Managing water use, emissions, biodiversity impact, and energy efficiency in operations.
  • Human Rights and Labor: Ensuring fair labor practices, health and safety, and grievance mechanisms.
  • Supply Chain Transparency: Promoting traceability and responsible sourcing from upstream partners.
  • Governance & Business Integrity: Anti-corruption, legal compliance, and ethical business practices.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Continuous engagement with affected communities and indigenous groups.
  • Waste and Recycling: Strategies to minimize production waste and promote circular economy practices.
  • Audit and Certification: Independent third-party assessment of conformity with ASI Performance or Chain of Custody Standards.

Why ASI Certification Matters

  • Demonstrates commitment to sustainable and ethical aluminium practices.
  • Improves transparency and accountability across the aluminium supply chain.
  • Builds stronger relationships with clients who prioritize sustainability.
  • Opens opportunities to participate in global sustainability-focused projects.
  • Reduces environmental impact and improves operational efficiency.
  • Supports alignment with global ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) goals.
  • Enhances brand reputation and investor confidence.

At Eurotech Canada, we guide aluminium producers, fabricators, and suppliers through the ASI certification journey, from system readiness assessments to third-party audit support.
